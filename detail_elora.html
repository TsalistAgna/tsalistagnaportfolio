Elora Wedding Organizer

Project Overview

Elora is a luxury wedding organizer social media mockup project designed to showcase how elegant visual storytelling can strengthen a premium brand presence on Instagram. The project explores the combination of minimalist layouts, soft romantic aesthetics, and cinematic wedding visuals to create a refined and emotionally engaging digital experience.

The visual direction focuses on simplicity, luxury, and timeless elegance. Neutral color palettes, serif typography, soft overlays, and spacious compositions were intentionally used to communicate exclusivity while maintaining a calm and sophisticated atmosphere throughout the feed.

This project includes multiple types of social media content such as carousel posts, single feed promotions, Instagram stories, and short-form video mockups. Each design was created to maintain visual consistency while serving different communication goals, ranging from emotional branding and wedding inspiration to promotional and engagement-based content.

Design Goal

Creating Elegant & Emotional Wedding Branding

The challenge was to create a wedding organizer social media identity that feels premium, emotionally engaging, and visually cohesive while still maintaining clarity across multiple content formats.

The feed needed to balance luxurious aesthetics with storytelling-driven communication. Every post was designed to highlight emotional moments, wedding experiences, and elegant event details while maintaining a strong and recognizable visual identity.

Creative Process

The design process focused on building a visually cohesive luxury wedding brand through elegant compositions, emotional storytelling, and premium social media content.

1

Moodboard & Visual Direction

Explored luxury wedding references, romantic photography styles, soft neutral palettes, and editorial-inspired layouts to establish Elora’s elegant and timeless visual identity.

2

Carousel Content Design

Designed educational and inspirational carousel posts focused on wedding details, venue atmosphere, floral styling, lighting, and table decoration using layered imagery and elegant typography compositions.

3

Single Feed Promotions

Created single-post promotional layouts highlighting wedding moments, venue ambience, and emotional storytelling through cinematic imagery and spacious visual hierarchy.

4

Instagram Story Experience

Designed interactive Instagram Story concepts to create a more immersive audience experience, including wedding reveal countdowns, POV storytelling, and event announcement visuals.

5

Short-form Video Mockups

Developed luxury wedding video post concepts inspired by cinematic short-form content to enhance emotional engagement and strengthen the premium storytelling experience.

Key Design Focus

  • Built a luxury wedding organizer visual identity system.
  • Created cohesive social media layouts across multiple content formats.
  • Applied emotional storytelling through elegant visual compositions.
  • Maintained visual consistency using typography, spacing, and soft color palettes.
  • Designed engagement-focused content while preserving premium branding aesthetics.
