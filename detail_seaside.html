Seaside Lodge Social Media & Brochure Mockup

Project Overview

Seaside Lodge is a social media and brochure mockup case created to explore how visual branding and Instagram feed design can communicate the atmosphere, identity, and guest experience of a tropical hospitality destination.

The project focuses on building a calm, luxurious, and nature-inspired digital presence through cohesive visual storytelling. Inspired by seaside retreats and tropical landscapes, the design direction combines warm earthy tones, elegant typography, cinematic imagery, and soft overlays to create a relaxing and premium visual experience.

Through this mockup case, I aimed to demonstrate how social media visuals can strengthen hospitality branding while maintaining audience engagement and aesthetic consistency across multiple posts.

Design Direction

The overall visual identity was designed to evoke feelings of comfort, exclusivity, and tranquility. To achieve this atmosphere, I used muted tropical color palettes, spacious layouts, elegant serif typography, and immersive photography compositions inspired by luxury resort branding.

Each feed post was designed with a different communication objective while still maintaining a unified visual language. Decorative overlays, balanced spacing, and soft lighting treatments were carefully arranged to enhance the emotional storytelling without overwhelming the content.

Feed Breakdown

1

Brand Introduction Post

The first post introduces the visual identity of Seaside Lodge by highlighting the natural scenery and peaceful atmosphere through cinematic photography and spacious typography composition.

2

Facilities Showcase

The second post focuses on presenting lodge facilities and guest experiences through lifestyle-oriented visuals, combining informative content with immersive imagery to create a more engaging hospitality presentation.

3

Promotional Campaign

The final post was designed as a promotional campaign highlighting special weekday offers using stronger visual hierarchy, promotional elements, and clear call-to-action placement while maintaining aesthetic consistency across the feed.

4

Lodge Brochure

The brochure design reflects the lodge vibe with clear information to attract customer.

Design Focus

Throughout the design process, I focused on balancing promotional communication with emotional storytelling to create a feed that feels both visually appealing and strategically engaging.

Typography choices were selected to reinforce the premium hospitality atmosphere, while layout compositions were carefully arranged to maintain readability without reducing the visual impact of the photography.

This project serves as a social media mockup case study that demonstrates my approach in creating visually cohesive digital branding for hospitality-focused content.
